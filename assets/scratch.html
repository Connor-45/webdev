This is the GOAT

Michael Jordan is widely considered the greatest basketball player of all time due to his unmatched dominance during the 1990s. He led the Chicago Bulls to six NBA championships, earning Finals MVP each time, and maintained a perfect 6–0 Finals record. His killer instinct, clutch performances, scoring ability, and defensive prowess set a standard that remains unmatched. While LeBron James is an incredible all-around player, Jordan's legacy of dominance, influence, and competitive fire continues to set him apart.
